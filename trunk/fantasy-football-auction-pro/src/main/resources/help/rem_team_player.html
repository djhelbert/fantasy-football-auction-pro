Remove Player from Team

A player can be removed from a Team in two different ways.

Steps
  • Select the Fantasy Teams tab.
  • Highlight the player to be removed from the fantasy team.
  • Right click on the player name.
  • Select the Remove menu item.
  • The player is automatically removed from the team roster and is now available again.
Steps
  • Select the Team Owners tab.
  • Highlight the fantasy team of the player.
  • Highlight the player to be removed from the fantasy team.
  • Click the Remove Player button from the tool bar.
  • The player is automatically removed from the team roster and is now available again.
