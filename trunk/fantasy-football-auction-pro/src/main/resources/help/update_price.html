Update Price

A player auction price can be updated two different ways.

Steps
  • Select the Fantasy Teams tab.
  • Highlight the player to be updated from the fantasy team.
  • Right click on the player name.
  • Select the Update menu item.
  • Select the new player auction amount from the price list.
  • Click the Ok button.
Steps
  • Select the Team Owners tab.
  • Highlight the fantasy team of the player.
  • Highlight the player to be updated from the fantasy team.
  • Double click the Price column and enter the new amount. Click the enter button when complete.
  • The player amount is automatically updated.
